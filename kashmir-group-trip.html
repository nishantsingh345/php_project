DAY 1 - TOUCHDOWN AT SRINAGAR!

  • Tour Delivery Team welcomes you at pick- up point, hotel transfer and check-in
  • Take an evening walk and sunset Shikara Ride at the beautiful Dal Lake
  • Come back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Srinagar

Dal Lake is the most popular attractions in Srinagar surrounded by a splendid view on all sides. The floating vegetable and flower market in the lake is a must-see!

DAY 2- THE SERENITY OF SRINAGAR

  • Breakfast at hotel, proceed for Shankaracharya Temple trek
  • Lunch at a local restaurant, drive to Mughal Gardens & Pari Mahal
  • Head back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Srinagar

Shankaracharya Temple is amongst the top religious attractions in Srinagar, named after the great philosopher Shankarcharya. The temple offers a panoramic view of Srinagar City, accessible by a staircase of 243 steps.

One of India’s most beautiful Mughal Gardens, here is a perfect representation of Persian-influenced Mughal architecture in its prime amidst the spectacular mountainous setting.

Literally translating to ‘palace of fairies’, Pari Mahal sits pretty above the Zabarwan mountain range, overlooking the Dal Lake and offering a stunning view of all of Srinagar.

DAY 3- VISIT DOODHPATHRI

  • Early breakfast at the hotel, followed by a short road trip to Doodhpathri
  • Chase an adrenalin rush with ATV rides and horse rides (ride cost not included in the package)
  • Breathe in the scent of flowers at Tosa Maidan! Settle down for a picnic lunch with local Kashmiri cuisine
  • Visit Shaliganga river, a hidden gem flowing through meadows and mountains
  • Back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Srinagar

The Ashtaar glacier gives birth to 2 rivers- Shaliganga and Doodhganga. The waters of these rivers hit rocky paths along the way, appearing ‘milky’ and thus inspiring the name Doodhpathri, the Valley of Milk!

DAY 4- HEIGHTS OF SINTHAN TOP

  • Early breakfast at the hotel, drive to SinthanTop for sightseeing
  • Enjoy the snow, adventure enthusiasts can choose from activities like Paragliding, horse riding, rock climbing and skiing (ride cost not included in the package)
  • After lunch, proceed to Pahalgam, check-in and overnight stay at Pahalgam

Towering at a height of 3,800m (12414 feet), Sinthan Top is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the region. Covered in snow throughout the year, this location offers gorgeous 360° views of the Jammu and Kashmir regions.

DAY 5- GET WOWED AT PAHALGAM!

  • Wake up to beautiful views, leave after breakfast for sightseeing in ABC valley
  • Aru Valley - 11km from Pahalgam, this is the base camp for treks, hikes and horse riding to Kolahoi Glacier and the Tarsar - Marsar Lake
  • Betaab Valley - Encircled by tall deodar trees and pine forests, it is famous for the movie Betaab which was shot here, hence the name
  • Chandanwari - Starting point of the Amarnath Yatra, you can enjoy snow sledding on a snow bridge here or a snowy picnic!
  • Overnight stay in Pahalgam

Pahalgam also known as ‘Village of Shepherds’ in Kashmiri is a popular tourist destination and hill station. Situated at an altitude of 7200 ft (2200m), its lush green meadows and pristine waters attract thousands of tourists from across the world each year.
